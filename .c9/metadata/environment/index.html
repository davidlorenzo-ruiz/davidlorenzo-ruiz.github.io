","\t\t\t
Super Mario Crossover 2 is a the sequel flash game to Super Mario Crossover and continues the great idea of combining all sorts of NES characters into one flash game. Choose between: Mario, Luigi, Link, Samus, Simon, Bill, Bass, Ryu, Megaman, or Sophia III. Each character will perform and act just like they do in their own video games. In Super Mario Crossover 2, some extra modes have been added including a cool Survial Mode where each character becomes unusable if they die. They can revive by spending a life but only after entering a new world (i.e. moving from 1-3 to 2-1).
